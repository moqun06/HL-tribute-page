Hedy Lamarr

Actress and inventor

We can say without a semblance of a doubt that she was a woman who had an interesting life, She was curious, intelligent and talented. She travelled and changed residence many times from a young age. She was the first to do many things on the big screen when films were still made in black and white she was a star. But she also had a great and resourceful mind, thta made her learn from everything available around her; she did experiments and made inventions. One of which is very present in our daily life nowadays


Some facts about her :

  • She was a Film star from 1930's to 1950's
  • Heddy was often know as: "The most Beautiful Woman in Films"
  • Durinng WWII along with George Antheil(pianist,author and inventor) developed a radio guidance technological system. Same system that is currently incorporated into Wi-Fi, CDMA and Bluetooth
  • The U.S. Navy did not adopt the technology at the time
  • It was not used until 1962, by that time their patent was no longer valid
  • Became an American citizen in 1953
  • She married and divorced 6 times
  • Died in USA in 2000
